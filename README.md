# Addis Alem Reise Adbarat Wegedamat Debre Tsion Mariam Church Website

An informative landing page dedicated to the historic **Addis Alem Mariam Church** in Ethiopia. This project showcases the spiritual legacy, architectural beauty, and historical significance of the site established by Emperor Menelik II.

## üåü Overview

This website serves as a digital gateway for pilgrims, historians, and travelers to explore the "Dagmit Tsion" (Second Zion) of Addis Alem. It highlights the church's transition from a potential royal capital to a major spiritual hub.

### Key Sections:

- **Sacred Legacy:** History of the church and its connection to Emperor Menelik II.
- **Architecture:** Details on the repurposed Throne Hall and vivid religious murals.
- **Church Museum:** Information on imperial crowns, robes, and ancient manuscripts.
- **Historical Context:** Exploration of the surrounding 1900s-era houses and the religious school.
- **Interactive Booking:** A functional "Book Your Visit" feature powered by JavaScript.
